FEATURED PROJECTS

MICRO -
AGGRESSIONS IN MEDICINE

MICROAGGRESSIONS IN MEDICINE

What are the effects of microaggressions in clinical medicine, and how can practitioners avoid them?

HIV IN AFRICAN AMERICAN ELDERS

Exploring the intersections of race, HIV-related stigma, ageism, and service delivery for HIV patients. What are the impacts of a theater-based intervention in reducing HIV stigma?

BLACK MEN & CHILDREN'S LITERACY SUCCESS

MIDDLE SCHOOL SCIENCE CITIZENS

Developing partnerships and pedagogy to enable teachers and students to do collaborative studies of climate change across the globe.

HOUSING JUSTICE IN LOUISVILLE & BEYOND

Developing and advocating for policies to improve access to safe, fair, and affordable housing.

PROJECT STAAR

Improving the ability of systems to effectively respond to human trafficking in a trauma-informed manner. How can we reduce stigma and promote resilience amongst survivors?

PROJECT UNCAGED

How do trauma-informed writing workshops affect girls in Youth Detention Services? How can their writing be used to engage policy makers and the media about juvenile justice reform?
