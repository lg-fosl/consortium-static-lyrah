CONSORTIUM FUNDING OPPORTUNITY - 2020 CALL FOR PROPOSALS

The Consortium is pleased to offer 2020 funding to transdisciplinary social justice research (TDR) teams led by UofL faculty. The Consortium is offering mini-grants and student funding for the initiation or completion of transdisciplinary social justice research or creative activity project that is community-engaged. Available funds are more limited than in the first three-year cycle, as the Consortium is now sustained by continued support from deans, departments, and other UofL offices (such as the HSC Office of Diversity and others).
